INSIGHTS FROM SALES DASHBOARD (TASK 8)

1. The West region had the highest total sales among all regions, particularly showing strong performance during the third quarter (Q3).

2. The Technology category consistently outperformed Furniture and Office Supplies in terms of revenue, indicating strong customer demand for tech products.

3. Sales saw a noticeable peak in November and December, suggesting increased customer spending during the holiday season.

4. The Central region, while showing consistent sales, lagged behind the East and West in overall revenue contribution.
